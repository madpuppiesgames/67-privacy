ACCOUNT & DATA DELETION

APP: 67 RUNNER · DEVELOPER: MAD PUPPIES · LAST UPDATED: 16 JULY 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your account data associated with the game 67 Runner (package com.runner67).

1 HOW TO REQUEST DELETION

Email [email protected] with:

  • Subject line: Delete my 67 Runner data
  • Your in-game display name (the nickname shown on the leaderboard), and
  • if available, your Google Play Games account used to sign in.

We use this information to locate and remove your leaderboard entry. We will confirm by email once the deletion is complete.

2 WHAT DATA IS DELETED

On request, we delete all account data we hold about you, namely:

  • Your display name (nickname)
  • Your submitted scores / distances
  • Your player identifiers (random player ID and Google Play Games ID)
  • Any cloud-saved game progress stored through Google Play Games Saved Games

3 WHAT DATA IS RETAINED

We do not retain personal account data after deletion. Anonymous, aggregated records that cannot identify you may remain. Residual copies in encrypted backups are purged on our normal backup rotation within 90 days.

4 DELETION TIMEFRAME

Requests are processed within 30 days of receipt.

5 DELETING LOCAL DATA YOURSELF

Game progress and settings stored on your device are removed immediately when you uninstall the app or clear its data in your device settings.
